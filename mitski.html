'Bury At Makeout Creek' was released in 2014. The 24-year-old broke her 9 track album's streak with this 10 track album. This album's "crashout" track is 'Drunk Walk Home', coming in with the usage of foul language, explosive bass sounds, and gutteral screams in the song. The artist ends the album with arguably one of her saddest tracks, 'Last Words of a Shooting Star', expressing morbid yet calm lyrics of acceptance of death as it ends.
